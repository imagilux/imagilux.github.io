## Why Imagilux

The stance and principles that shape every product decision below.

- **[The Light Out era →](/platform/light-out/)** — Self-operating infrastructure, end-to-end. The design constraint behind every directive and default.
- **[Open by design →](/platform/open-by-design/)** — Vendor-neutral specs, first-party SDKs, reference implementations under permissive licenses.
- **[Trust model →](/platform/trust-model/)** — Signed images and verifiable provenance; compliance as a property of the system.

## Architecture

Three components, designed to compose.

- **[Universal Machine Format →](/products/umf/)** — The open specification for universal machine images.
- **[Boreal control plane →](/products/boreal/)** — The autonomous control plane for fleet-scale lifecycle.
- **[Imagilux SDK →](/platform/sdk/)** — First-party SDKs in eight languages.
