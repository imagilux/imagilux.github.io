## What we do

Imagilux delivers two complementary products that together remove operator toil from the infrastructure lifecycle:

- **[UMF](/products/umf/)** — the open specification for building universal machine images. Parse once, deploy anywhere.
- **[BOREAL](/products/boreal/)** — the autonomous control plane for fleet-scale lifecycle management.

Together they let teams declare intent and step away. Compliance, patching, and lifecycle convergence happen without a human in the loop.

## Why Light Out

Modern infrastructure operators don't lack capability — they lack leverage. Every fleet still ships with hand-rolled images, drift between environments, and a queue of upgrade tickets that never empties. Light Out is the design stance that solves this at the root: reproducible, signed images flowing into self-healing fleets that converge to declared state without manual intervention.

## Our principles

1. **Open by default.** Specifications, SDKs, and reference implementations are first-party open work. No proprietary lock-in at the substrate.
2. **Sovereignty-respecting.** Air-gapped, regulated, and edge deployments are first-class — not bolt-ons.
3. **Trust through provenance.** Every layer is signed and verifiable end-to-end. Compliance is a property of the system, not a process bolted on top.
4. **Operator-grade tooling.** Built by people who have been on call.
